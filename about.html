ABOUT Us

"Aplify" is a unique platform, designed exclusively to cater for specific needs and requirements of the people by facilitating a convenient and secured e-platform for carrying out hassle-free and worry-free Customer-to-Customer (C2C) & Business-to-Custmer (B2C) transactions with full of ease and comfort.


"Aplify" is the first-ever e-platform of the Digital India that brings a unique community feature, i.e., “Armed Forces”, specially customised to provide dedicated services to the members and families of the Indian Armed Forces (both serving & retired).

With this exclusive feature, users belonging to this community may post Ad/ buy & sell/ browse/ filter/ search/ avail the services within the community.

This platform not only provides services to the Armed Forces personnel, but also to all other citizens of India. To avail the same, users may register themselves under the community “Others”. We assure that the services rendered by us to any kind of users (either from the Community "Armed Forces" or "Others") will be at par and excellent. We deliver nation-wide coverage so as to cater for the requirements of the people living in different areas of India.
